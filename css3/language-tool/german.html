Basic Language Learning Aid

Below I created a simple example of the kind of applications that can be created with CSS3 animations. They are flash cards of the type that are often used when people are learning a new language.

The animation is achieved using CSS3 3D transforms and transitions, more specifically I applied a 180 degree rotation around the Y axis and the tranistion is set to rotate the card in 0.5 seconds(supported by major browsers except IE).

The aim here is for the first set of ten cards is to test your vocabulary by predicting the image behind the cards based on the German words, left click the card you want and hold down to reveal the image behind it.

Der Hund
Das Haus
Der Baum
Der Mond
Das Pferd
Die Kirche
Das Flugzeug
Die Blume
Das Auto
Der Berg

The follwing ten cards work in an opposite way to the previous ten, here the idea is to see if you can figure out the German word from the image.

Like before left click on the one you want and hold down to reveal the word.

Die Zeitung
Der Wald
Das Krankenhaus
Der Bär
Der Zug
Die Schlange
Die Sonne
Das Schiff
Der Regenbogen
Der Schmetterling
