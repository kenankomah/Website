Basic Language Learning Aid

Below I created a simple example of the kind of applications that can be created with CSS3 animations. They are flash cards of the type that are often used when people are learning a new language.

The animation is achieved using CSS3 3D transforms and transitions, more specifically I applied a 180 degree rotation around the Y axis and the tranistion is set to rotate the card in 0.5 seconds(supported by major browsers except IE).

The aim here is for the first set of ten cards is to test your vocabulary by predicting the image behind the cards based on the Spanish words, left click the card you want and hold down to reveal the image behind it.

El Perro
La Casa
El Árbol
La Luna
El Caballo
La Iglesia
El Avión
La Flor
El Coche
La Montaña

The follwing ten cards work in an opposite way to the previous ten, here the idea is to see if you can figure out the Spanish word from the image.

Like before left click on the one you want and hold down to reveal the word.

El Periódico
El Bosque
El Hospital
El Oso
El Tren
La Serpiente
El Sol
La Nave
El Arco Iris
La Mariposa
